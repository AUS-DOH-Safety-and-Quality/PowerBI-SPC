Capabilities

Our visual supports a wide range of chart types and features, with an emphasis on flexibility and customisability. If there's anything that it can't do for you, just let us know!

Chart Types:

  • run: Run Chart
  • i: Individual Measurements (AKA XmR)
  • i_m: Individual Measurements - Median Centerline
  • i_mm: Individual Measurements - Median Centerline and Median Moving Range Limits
  • mr: Moving Range of Individual Measurements
  • p: Proportions
  • p': Proportions with Large-Sample Correction
  • u: Rates
  • u': Rates with Large-Sample Correction
  • c: Counts
  • xbar: Sample Means
  • s: Sample SDs
  • g: Number of Non-events Between Events
  • t: Time Between Events

Features:

  • Evaluate patterns across multiple processes with a summary table
  • Customise aesthetics for all aspects of the plot
  • Apply conditional formatting for process- or point-specific customisation
  • Use the MDC icons to highlight variation and assurance in your data
  • Export to PDF and PowerPoint
  • Export calculated control limits from visual
  • Label individual points on charts
  • Provide custom control limits (specification limits)
